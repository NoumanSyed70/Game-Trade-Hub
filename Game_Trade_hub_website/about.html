About Game Trade Hub

Game Trade Hub is a front-end-only marketplace designed for Pakistani gamers to buy, sell, and trade gaming-related products. Our platform provides a seamless experience for listing and discovering games, consoles, accessories, and more.

Our Mission

  • Support the local gaming community in Pakistan
  • Promote e-sports through tournament listings
  • Provide a smooth, front-end experience for trading gaming products
  • Showcase the potential of front-end development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Meet the Developers

Game Trade Hub was created by a Software Engineering student (L1F23BSSE0216, L1F23BSSE0370, L1F23BSSE0387) as an academic project. This platform is designed for demonstration and testing purposes, featuring an admin-only login system without a backend.

The project showcases a passion for gaming and clean front-end development practices.
